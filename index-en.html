✦ Features

Everything you need to
take control of your money

No spreadsheets, no hassle. Fintab organizes your finances in a simple, visual way.

💰

Expense Tracking

Record income and expenses with custom categories. Know exactly where every cent of your money goes.

🔔

Auto Detection

The app reads bank notifications and automatically suggests entries. Your data never leaves your phone.

🔁

Recurring Transactions

Set up recurring bills and income — the app logs them automatically so you never miss a payment.

❤️

Financial Health

Get a clear snapshot of your financial situation with indicators that show where you stand and how to improve.

📁

Import & Export

Bring data from other sources or export your records as CSV anytime. Your data, your freedom.

🔒

PIN Lock

Protect app access with a personal PIN. An extra layer of security for your financial information.

📊

Annual View PRO

Analyze your spending month by month across the entire year and spot financial patterns with clear charts.

👛

Multiple Wallets PRO

Organize your money by wallet: checking, savings, credit card, and whatever else you need.

🎯

Budgets PRO

Set monthly limits by category and track in real time whether you're staying on plan.

🏆

Financial Goals PRO

Set savings targets, track progress visually, and achieve your goals with greater clarity.

👥

Sharing PRO

Manage finances together with a partner, family, or business partner — all synced in real time.
